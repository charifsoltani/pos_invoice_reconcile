POS Partial Invoice and refund

Keep track of client payments && debts

Features
  • Invoice unpaid order or partially paid orders
  • Reconcile payment of orders and invoices
  • Create stock picking

Invoice unpaid or partially paid order

keep the invoice open with balance

You can invoice an order that is not fully paid.

When client don't pay the whole Order we need to register what he paid. by invoicing his Order save payments on that invoice and keep it open, so when client come back next time he can pay his debts.

Support for refunds

create refund for product return

When client return product an ask for invoice the type of invoice should be refund.

When client return products and ask for invoice, you invoice the order and you can pay the whole amount in negative or part of it. and you cannot validate the order only if you specify the whole amount in negative.

Reconcile journal entries

reconcile invoice and order payments

When closing and validating the session this module automatically reconcile the entries and mark invoice that are fully paid as paid, and the rest as open with new balance if the client pay part of the mount.

As you can see Invoice still open with balance of 2000.00 and in payment tabs you can see the list of payment that are maid by the client. invoiced return are saved in as refunds.
